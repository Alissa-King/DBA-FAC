FAC Single-Audit Findings Prediction

Logistic regression and gradient boosting trained on Federal Audit Clearinghouse data to screen organizations at risk of audit findings before results close.

DBA Research — Forward-Year Holdout
About this project
What is FAC?Single Audit data from the Federal Audit Clearinghouse covers every organization spending ≥$750K in federal funds per year. Findings indicate financial, compliance, or internal-control weaknesses.
Why forward-year testing?A random split lets the model “see the future.” We train on earlier years and test on the most recent year — the honest analogue of scoring an organization before its audit closes. TimeSeriesSplit cross-validation gives the same guarantee over multiple folds.
What is SHAP?SHapley Additive exPlanations assign each feature a contribution to each prediction. Global importance = mean |SHAP| across all test audits. Per-audit cards show exactly why the model flagged each organization.
Feature setsPROFILE uses organization type, spending size band, and low-risk status. ENRICHED adds number of federal programs, prior-year finding count, and cognizant oversight agency.
Datasets shownPILOT = one page of real FAC data (2022 train / 2023 test, ~5K rows). DEMO = synthetic data mirroring FAC schema (4 training years, 4K rows/year) with known ground truth for methods validation.
Class imbalanceFindings are rare in real FAC data (~10% base rate). Models use class_weight=‘balanced’ by default. The threshold is tuned to hit 80% recall; precision is the cost paid for that coverage.
